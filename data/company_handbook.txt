Aurora Robotics - Company Handbook (Sample)

About the company:
Aurora Robotics was founded in 2019 to build small warehouse robots that move shelves
instead of having workers walk to them. The company grew from 4 engineers to 60 employees
by 2023 and now operates in 3 countries: Pakistan, the UAE, and Germany.

Product - The A-200 Shelf-Bot:
The A-200 is Aurora's main product. It can carry up to 200kg, navigates a warehouse using
floor markers combined with onboard cameras, and recharges automatically at docking stations
placed every 50 meters. A single A-200 can run for about 8 hours before needing a recharge.

Research team:
In 2024, Aurora started a dedicated research team. Their focus is building a robotic arm
attachment so the A-200 can restock shelves automatically instead of just moving them.
Early prototypes can place small boxes onto a shelf with about 90% accuracy.

Customer support policy:
Customers with an active support contract can reach the support team via email at
support@aurorarobotics.example or through the in-app chat. Standard response time is
4 business hours. Robots under warranty are repaired free of charge within 30 days of
a reported issue.

Office locations:
- Lahore, Pakistan (engineering HQ)
- Dubai, UAE (regional sales office)
- Berlin, Germany (EU operations)
